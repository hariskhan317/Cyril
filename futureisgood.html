Podcast description

Edit

Lean from Woud is a comfortable and minimalist lounge chair, characterized by an angular backrest. The generous seat and backrest are upholstered with Gabriel’s high-quality Luna 2 wool fabric and rest on slender metal legs. Created by Danish design studio NUR, Lean invites you take a seat, lean back and relax. Plus, the simple and elegant design makes a covetable addition to any modern space and decor.
